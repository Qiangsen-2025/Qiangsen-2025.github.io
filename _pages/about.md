I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the Faculty of Computing, Harbin Institute of Technology. My primary research interests lie in computer vision, deep learning, remote sensing, and their applications in smart agriculture. I am privileged to be advised by Prof. Ran Meng and am affiliated with the National Key Laboratory of Smart Farm Technologies and Systems.

This page serves as a living record of my ongoing research—including conceptual ideas, methodological reflections, experimental progress, and preliminary findings. By maintaining full transparency throughout the research process, I aim to address the fundamental question of how to build a framework that genuinely invites external input and collaboration. It is my hope that this open record will facilitate timely feedback and constructive guidance from both faculty members and fellow researchers.

My Google Scholar profile is available at [Li Xu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=z694zb0AAAAJ&hl=en)
